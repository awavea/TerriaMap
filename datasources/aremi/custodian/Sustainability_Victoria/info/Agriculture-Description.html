The core dataset used in determining baseline production quantities for the primary production sector is the ABS Agricultural Commodities datasets. ABS data has been used from 2010-11 to 2015-16, to provide a time-series average of production from a variety of agricultural commodities.

Residue factors were applied to the six year average production values based on desktop research and industry consultation and feedback. This includes the following:

  • Cereals – straw and chaff
  • Non-Cereals – oilseed pods
  • Cull Fruit
  • Cull Vegetable
  • Dairy Manure
  • Piggery Manure
  • Poultry Litter

The results provide estimations of residue quantities generated based on a six-year average of primary production data.
